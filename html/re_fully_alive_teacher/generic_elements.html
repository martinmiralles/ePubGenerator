[Use the "poem" element whenever you require a passage of text (i.e., prayer, hymn, poem) to have specific line breaks. By default the content within the poem element will be italicized. Make sure to indicate which lines on the markup do not need to be italicized.]

Poem 01:

[Following is the style for "poem 01". (Some of the lines are italicized.)]

Lord, open my eyes to see those who hunger so that I may share my food.
Open my heart to the lonely and ignored so that I may share my time.
Open my spirit to receive your charity so that I can share it with others. May I see a need, lend a hand, and touch a heart, one person at a time, knowing that each one I serve is You. Amen.
